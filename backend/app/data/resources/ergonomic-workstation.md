# Ergonomic workstation envelope

## 1. Work surface and height — NF EN 527-1:2011

**NF EN 527-1:2011** — "Office furniture — Work tables and desks — Part 1:
Dimensions" — sets dimensional requirements for desks used in seated,
sit-stand, and standing positions. It does not cover storage units or
reception desks (those are in other standards).

The standard defines :

- **Minimum and maximum worktop height** measured at the front edge
- **Adjustment range** recorded for height-adjustable desks
- **Free knee and leg space** under the worktop — a template of fixed depth
  and width, variable height, defines the minimum clear zone

### Worktop heights

| Use case                       | Height at front edge                        |
|--------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Fixed-height seated desk (legacy) | **720 mm ± 10 mm** `[À VÉRIFIER contre 527-1:2011 exacte]` |
| Sit-stand height-adjustable    | Typical range **650 – 1250 mm**             |
| Standing-only                  | 950 – 1250 mm selon stature                 |

The 650 – 1250 mm range is the de facto industry standard for electric
sit-stand desks (e.g. Steelcase Migration SE, Herman Miller Jarvis) and
corresponds to the 5th – 95th percentile P5/P95 adult European stature
range.

### Worktop depth and width

| Dimension              | Minimum recommended                        |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Depth (front → back)   | **≥ 800 mm** for DSE work with dual screen |
| Depth (alternative)    | 600 – 700 mm acceptable for single screen  |
| Width (side-to-side)   | **≥ 1 200 mm** for a single occupant       |
| Width for dual screen  | **≥ 1 600 mm**                              |

### Free leg space under worktop

- Knee clearance : depth **≥ 600 mm** at knee height, **≥ 800 mm** at foot
  level `[À VÉRIFIER valeurs exactes 527-1]`
- Width between desk legs : **≥ 600 mm** (practical minimum), **800 mm** for
  comfort

## 2. Clearance around workstations

Typical planning clearances, consistent with industry guidance :

| Context                                    | Clearance                  |
|--------------------------------------------|----------------------------|
| Front of the desk (chair pull-out + user)  | **≥ 1 200 mm**             |
| Side passage between two parallel desks    | **≥ 900 mm**               |
| Circulation corridor serving several desks | **≥ 1 200 – 1 400 mm**     |

## 3. Chair ergonomics

- **Seat height adjustable** : 40 – 52 cm (5th – 95th percentile)
- **Lumbar support** adjustable in height and depth
- **Armrests** adjustable in height, width, and ideally depth
- **Back tilt** independent of seat, with lockable range
- **Swivel** 360°, 5-star base with castors appropriate to the floor finish
- Reference : EN 1335-1/2/3 for office chair dimensions, strength, and
  safety `[À VÉRIFIER édition en vigueur]`

## 4. Display screen equipment (DSE)

- **Viewing distance** : 50 – 70 cm (screen ~eye distance)
- **Top of screen at or slightly below eye level**
- **Screen tilt** 10 – 20° back from vertical
- **Brightness** matched to surrounding surface luminance to avoid adaptation
  strain
- **No direct reflection** of windows or overhead luminaires on screen — a
  common planning mistake, addressed by orienting desks **perpendicular to
  the window plane**

## 5. Lighting

Two regimes coexist :

### 5.1 Legal floor — code du travail

| Zone                                       | Minimum (lux)            |
|--------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| Work premises (general)                    | **120**                  |
| Office without natural light               | **200**                  |
| Circulations with permanent occupancy      | **60** `[À VÉRIFIER]`    |

Reference : Code du travail, articles **R. 4223-1 à R. 4223-12**. Natural
light must be prioritised (R. 4223-3) ; artificial lighting is a complement.

### 5.2 Engineering best practice — NF EN 12464-1:2021

| Location                                   | Maintained illuminance Ēm (lux) |
|--------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Writing, typing, reading, data processing  | **500**                        |
| Conference / meeting rooms                 | **500**                        |
| Technical drawing                          | **750**                        |
| Reception, cashier                         | **300** `[À VÉRIFIER]`         |
| Archive, storage                           | **200**                        |

**Context modifiers** in EN 12464-1:2021 raise the required level by one or
two steps (e.g. 500 → 750 lx) for :

- Workers aged 50+
- Visually demanding tasks
- High error-cost tasks

Other EN 12464-1 quality measures :

- **Unified Glare Rating (UGR)** : ≤ 19 in office
- **Color Rendering Index (Ra/CRI)** : ≥ 80, preferably ≥ 90 for design work
- **Uniformity** U₀ ≥ 0.60 on task area

## 6. Thermal environment

- **Operative temperature** : 20 – 24 °C winter, 23 – 26 °C summer (code du
  travail does not set hard numbers; these are AFNOR / ASHRAE 55 ranges,
  `[À VÉRIFIER]`)
- **Relative humidity** : 30 – 70 %
- **Air velocity** at occupant : ≤ 0.2 m/s to avoid draughts
- Reference : **NF EN ISO 7730** — ergonomics of the thermal environment
  (PMV/PPD indices) `[À VÉRIFIER édition]`

## 7. Indoor air quality and ventilation

- **Code du travail R. 4222-1 à R. 4222-26** : general ventilation
  obligations
- Minimum fresh air intake for office work : **25 m³/h/occupant** `[À
  VÉRIFIER contre dernière mise à jour R. 4222]`
- **CO₂** : < 1 000 ppm target, > 1 500 ppm triggers investigation
- **PM2.5** : < 10 µg/m³ (WHO 2021 guideline) `[À VÉRIFIER]`

## 8. Planning checklist

- [ ] Desks ≥ 1 600 × 800 mm for dual screen
- [ ] ≥ 1 200 mm front clearance + ≥ 900 mm side passage
- [ ] Sit-stand desks electrically adjustable 650 – 1 250 mm for ≥ 25 % of
  seats `[À VÉRIFIER politique client]`
- [ ] Task area 500 lx maintained, UGR ≤ 19, CRI ≥ 80
- [ ] Desks perpendicular to fenestration plane
- [ ] Operative temp 20 – 24 °C with ≥ 30 – 70 % RH
- [ ] ≥ 25 m³/h/occupant fresh air
