# Office furniture — brand reference

## 1. Height-adjustable desks

### Steelcase — Migration SE

Electric sit-stand desk, positioned at mid-to-upper market.

- **Rectangular depth (EMEA)** : 800 mm
- **Widths (EMEA)** : 1 200 / 1 400 / 1 600 / 1 800 / 2 000 mm
- **North American depths** : 23" / 24" / 29" / 30" / 35" / 36" on T-leg
- **North American widths** : 46" – 76" (configurable by 1/16" between 46" – 66")
- **Height range** : electric adjustment in the typical **650 – 1 250 mm**
  sit-stand range `[À VÉRIFIER spec sheet exact]`
- Source — [Steelcase Migration SE product page](https://www.steelcase.com/products/height-adjustable-desks/migration-se/)

### Herman Miller — Jarvis (formerly Fully Jarvis)

- **Rectangular sizes (rectangle top)** : 27" × 48" (686 × 1 219 mm),
  27" × 60" (686 × 1 524 mm), 30" × 72" (762 × 1 829 mm)
- **Height range** : **24.5" – 50" without top** (≈ 622 – 1 270 mm with a
  typical 25 mm top)
- **Lift speed** : 1.5 inch / second
- **Weight capacity** : 350 lbs (≈ 159 kg)
- **Acoustic noise of motor** : < 50 dB
- Source — [Herman Miller Jarvis product page](https://store.hermanmiller.com/standing-desks/jarvis-laminate-standing-desk/2548841.html)

### Vitra — Tyde 2

Sit-stand table family from Vitra, designed by Ronan and Erwan Bouroullec.

- Dimensions and height range `[À VÉRIFIER sur spec Vitra Tyde 2 2024-2025]`
- Positioned as design-forward premium; specify when the client brief
  prioritises identity and aesthetics.

### Humanscale — Float

Counterbalanced (non-electric) sit-stand desk, mechanical spring lift.

- Dimensions `[À VÉRIFIER sur spec Humanscale Float]`
- Advantage : no plug, no motor noise, no electronics failure

## 2. Task seating

### Steelcase — Series 1 / Series 2

Mid-market task chair. Series 1 is the entry, Series 2 adds air-mesh back.

- Seat height range : 406 – 521 mm `[À VÉRIFIER]`
- Fabric / mesh back options
- Source — manufacturer catalogue

### Herman Miller — Aeron

Industry reference ergonomic chair, 3 sizes (A/B/C).

- Size B (~80 % of adult population) : 3 heights, adjustable arms, PostureFit
  SL
- Weight capacity : 350 lbs (≈ 159 kg) on size B
- Source — [Herman Miller Aeron product page](https://store.hermanmiller.com/seating-office-chairs/aeron-chairs/) `[À VÉRIFIER URL]`

### Herman Miller — Embody

Premium ergonomic chair optimised for long DSE sessions.

### Vitra — ID Chair family

- Designed by Antonio Citterio
- Several backs : Mesh, Trim, Trim L, Cloud, Concept

### Wilkhahn — ON / IN

Known for 3D trimension (spine-friendly dynamic sitting).

## 4. Phone booths and pods

### Framery — One / One Compact

- **Framery One (standard)** : 1-person booth
  - External : ~**2 255 × 1 220 × 1 000 mm** (H × W × D)
- **Framery One Compact** : compact version
  - External : **2 250 × 1 030 × 1 000 mm** (H × W × D)
  - Interior table : 765 × 360 mm (W × D) at fixed 1 020 mm above floor
  - Seat : 380 × 380 mm, height-adjustable 590 – 850 mm
  - Required back-to-front clearance including doorway : **1 850 mm**
  - Minimum room height : **2 350 mm** (10 cm above pod)
- Acoustic claim : ~30 dB reduction (manufacturer claim)
- Source — [Framery One Compact tech specs](https://framery.com/en-us/office-pods-and-booths/framery-one-compact/tech-specs/)

## 9. Specification discipline

Before signing a furniture package :

- Every SKU has a **current spec sheet** in the project file, not just a URL
- Every dimension captured here is **confirmed against the spec sheet**
  (remove `[À VÉRIFIER]` only when you have the doc)
- **Lead times** ≥ 12 weeks for European premium brands from order
- **FF&E budget envelope** : Cat B French fit-out in 2024-2025 typically
  ranges 600 – 1 200 €/m² for furniture alone `[À VÉRIFIER contre bench
  local Saguez / Ubiq]`
