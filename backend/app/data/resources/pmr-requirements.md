# PMR / accessibility — ERP type W

**Legal base** : Arrêté du 20 avril 2017 relatif à l'accessibilité aux
personnes handicapées des établissements recevant du public lors de leur
construction et des installations ouvertes au public lors de leur aménagement
(Légifrance, JORFTEXT000034485459). Applicable to applications filed on or
after **1 July 2017**.

The arrêté of 8 December 2014 remains the applicable text for **existing
ERP** (cadre bâti existant). If the brief is a renovation, confirm which
regime applies.

## 1. General principle (Art. 1)

> Les personnes handicapées peuvent accéder à l'ensemble des locaux ouverts au
> public et en ressortir de manière autonome.

Autonomy is the bar : every programmed public-accessible space must be
independently reachable, traversable, and exitable by a person using a
wheelchair.

## 2. Clear widths of doors and circulations

| Element                                                    | Minimum clear width |
|------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| Principal door serving a zone of < 100 persons             | 0.90 m useful       |
| Principal door serving a zone of **≥ 100 persons**         | **1.40 m useful**   |
| Door of a non-adapted sanitary cabinet / individual cabin  | 0.77 m useful (0.80 m nominal)  |
| Main circulation corridor                                  | 1.40 m `[À VÉRIFIER dans le texte]`               |
| Secondary circulation / between workstations               | 0.90 m `[À VÉRIFIER]`                               |

**Practical planning consequence** : any collaborative zone (town hall, café)
that foreseeably holds ≥ 100 people must have 1.40 m clear doors — plan for
double-leaf openings early, do not discover this at permit stage.

## 3. Turning circle and manoeuvring

- **Turning circle** (wheelchair) : disk of **1.50 m diameter** required at
  the beginning and end of every circulation, in every sanitary cabinet
  adapted for PMR, and at every reception position.
- **Lateral transfer space** next to adapted WC : **0.80 m × 1.30 m** free
  area alongside the bowl.
- **Free approach** in front of any reception counter or adapted equipment :
  **0.90 m × 1.30 m** minimum.

## 4. Adapted sanitary cabinets

Every sanitary block open to the public must include at least one cabinet
adapted to PMR. Planning footprint for a single adapted cabinet :

- **Internal clear dimensions** : at least 1.50 m × 2.10 m `[À VÉRIFIER]`
- Bowl on one side, 0.80 × 1.30 m lateral transfer free on the other
- WC seat height 0.45 – 0.50 m, assisted grab bar on the transfer side
- Washbasin in the cabinet, rim at 0.70 – 0.85 m with knee clearance
- Flush, locking, light switch reachable from sitting position

## 5. Vertical circulation

- **Lifts** must be provided when there is a floor served at R+0 or below
  that is not reachable otherwise (details in Art. 7, 20 April 2017 arrêté).
  For ERP type W with ≥ 50 persons on upper floors, a PMR-accessible lift
  is mandatory. `[À VÉRIFIER seuil exact]`.
- **Stairs** must have contrasting nosings, a handrail on each side, and
  tactile warning strips at the top of flights.

## 6. Thresholds and level changes

- Any level change > 2 cm requires a ramp.
- Ramp slope : **≤ 5 %** preferred, **6 – 8 % tolerated** over short runs,
  **10 %** absolute maximum over ≤ 0.50 m.
- Horizontal landing of **1.40 m** every 10 m of ramp, and at every direction
  change.

## 7. Signage, floor contrast, tactile guidance

- Signage readable with 8° field of view from the approach path
- Luminance contrast ≥ 70 % between text and background, `[À VÉRIFIER
  valeur exacte dans arrêté]`
- Tactile guide paths on long open circulations
- Stair nosings contrasted and slip-resistant

## 8. Reception, counters, work surfaces

Adapted reception / counter :

- Part of the counter at **0.80 m** height max, knee clearance **≥ 0.70 m**
  wide, **≥ 0.60 m** deep, **0.70 m** floor-to-underside
- **0.90 × 1.30 m** free approach in front

## 9. Planning checklist (to run every brief against)

- [ ] At least one PMR-adapted entry
- [ ] Corridors ≥ 1.40 m main, ≥ 0.90 m secondary
- [ ] Doors ≥ 0.80 m everywhere, ≥ 1.40 m to high-capacity zones
- [ ] 1.50 m turning circle at every dead-end and inside every cabinet
- [ ] ≥ 1 adapted WC per sanitary block open to public
- [ ] Adapted reception in visitor areas
- [ ] Every level served by an accessible route (ramp ≤ 5 % or lift)
- [ ] Adapted parking : **2 %** minimum of total spaces (from `[À VÉRIFIER
      art exact]`), each space **3.30 m × 5.00 m**
